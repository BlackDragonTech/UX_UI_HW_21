About Able Academics

Able Academics School is a private institution located in Brevard County. Able provides both educational and behavioral services for students in VPK to Twelfth Grade. Our students range in skill and functional levels. Able Academics’ staff strives to create a unique learning environment that melds education and behavior services to meet students’ individual needs. We use a wide variety of assessments, pedagogical methods, and tender loving care.

Able Academics has created a curriculum program that follows the guidelines establish by the Florida Department of Education, utilizing both the Florida Content Standards and Florida Access Points Standards. Learn more about the Florida Standards and Access Points at www.floridastandards.org We use a wide variety of methods and teaching strategies to meet the individual needs of our students. Our program includes the following:

  • Reading
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Art
  • Physical Education
  • Music
  • Life Skills
